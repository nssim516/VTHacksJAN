Applications

There are both commercial and global applications for Aurelia technology. Take a look at some of them below!

Everyday Applications

Have you ever gone to unlock your phone in suboptimal lighting with Face ID and were met with [FACE ID DISABLED. PLEASE ENTER PASSWORD]? Yeah, us too. That's why Aurelia also has lots of applications in everyday life! These include more accurate facial readings in dark conditions so you will be able to use the technology in almost any lighting. Other applications include motion sensor techonology such as automatic sinks, soap dispensers, and even self-driving vehicles.

Commercial Applications in Professional Photography and Videography Services

Our tool can be used in professional environments to ensure accurate lighting conditions for ALL skin tones and inclusive media representation. Additionally, our technology could be implemented in online meeting platforms to help users present themselves better under different lighting situations during virtual calls.

Applications in the Classroom

Aurelia ensures that every student is on a level playing field through allowing the chosen device to pick up each student's features accurately. Some common applications for this in the classroom include increasing connection in virtual learning environments via higher face detection and proctored test applications such as LockDown Browser.

Colorism

Racial discrimination is a pervasive problem in the USA. As defined by Harvard, colorism, or skin color stratification, is a process that privileges light-skinned people of color over dark.

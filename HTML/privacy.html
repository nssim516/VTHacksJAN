Privacy

We respect user privacy and minimize data risks for all communities.

Aurelia is committed to user privacy protection.

At Aurelia Technologies, safeguarding your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your data is utilized are core values that drive our operations. We recognize the importance of building trust with our users, and we are deeply committed to protecting any personal information you provide. This includes facial data, which we manage with the highest standards of security and care. All data collection, storage, and processing activities are conducted in full compliance with ethical AI guidelines and the strict regulations outlined by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We take these measures to ensure that your personal information remains confidential, secure, and used only for the purposes you have consented to. Your privacy is not just a priority for us; it is a fundamental principle guiding every aspect of our technology and services.
